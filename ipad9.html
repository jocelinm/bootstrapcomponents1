iPad 9

Source: Swappa https://swappa.com/guide/apple-ipad-9th-gen/specs

  • The iPad 10.2-inch (officially iPad (9th generation), also known as iPad 9) is a tablet computer developed and marketed by Apple Inc. as the eighth-generation iPad's successor. On September 14, 2021, it was announced. The iPad (9th generation) has the following dimensions: 9.8" (H) x 6.8" (W) x 0.29" (D), with a 10.2" display. The WiFi model weighs 1.07 pounds, while the WiFi + Cellular model weighs 1.09 pounds. The iPad (9th generation) has the following dimensions: 9.8" (H) x 6.8" (W) x 0.29" (D), with a 10.2" display. The WiFi model weighs 1.07 pounds, while the WiFi + Cellular model weighs 1.09 pounds. The iPad (9th generation) has an 8MP wide-angle rear camera with an f/2.4 aperture, digital zoom up to 5x, and photo HDR. The rear camera can also record 1080pHD video (at 25 or 30 frames per second), slo-mo video (at 120 frames per second), and time-lapse video with stabilization. The front camera is a 12MP ultra-wide with Center Stage support. The front camera has an f/2.4 aperture, 2x zoom out, Retina Flash, HDR for photos, and 1080p HD video recording as key features (at 25, 30, or 60 fps). The iPad (9th generation) is powered by a 32.4Wh battery that can last up to 10 hours when surfing the web on WiFi or watching videos. This model can provide up to 9 hours of web surfing time when connected to a cellular network. This iPad has a Lightning charging connector like the ones that come with iPhones.
